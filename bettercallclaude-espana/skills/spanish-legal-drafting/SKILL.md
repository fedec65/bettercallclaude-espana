# Spanish Legal Drafting

## Objective
Draft precise, enforceable legal documents under Spanish law. Ensure compliance with mandatory provisions (Art. 6 CC, Art. 1255 CC, Art. 1101 CC), proper structural conventions, and court-specific formatting requirements.

## Document Types

### 1. Contracts (Contratos)
Standard structure:
1. **Partes** — Identification of parties (name, DNI/NIE/CIF, domicilio)
2. **Objeto** — Subject matter and purpose
3. **Obligaciones** — Mutual obligations and duties
4. **Precio** — Price, payment terms, and modalities
5. **Plazos** — Deadlines, duration, and renewal terms
6. **Garantías** — Warranties, securities, and guarantees
7. **Responsabilidad** — Liability clauses, limitation of liability, force majeure (fuerza mayor)
8. **Resolución** — Termination conditions and effects
9. **Ley aplicable y jurisdicción** — Governing law and dispute resolution (jurisdicción / arbitraje / mediación)
10. **Firma** — Signatures, date, and place

**Mandatory law compliance:**
- Art. 6 CC: Contracts must comply with mandatory law (normas imperativas).
- Art. 1255 CC: Freedom of contract within legal bounds.
- Art. 1101 CC: Liability for breach of contract (responsabilidad contractual).
- Art. 1895 CC: Limitation of liability clauses subject to good faith (buena fe).

### 2. Court Submissions (Escritos / Demandas)
Standard structure (LEC):
1. **Encabezamiento** — Court, parties, procedural identification
2. **Hechos** — Statement of facts in chronological order
3. **Fundamentos de Derecho** — Legal arguments with statutory and case law citations
4. **Pretensiones** — Specific relief sought (condena, declaración, etc.)
5. **Prueba** — Evidence offered (documental, testifical, pericial)
6. **Conclusión** — Summary request and signature

**Key rules:**
- Art. 399 LEC: Content requirements for the demanda
- Art. 401 LEC: Facts must be specific, clear, and concise
- Citations must be verifiable (STS, SAP, BOE references)

### 3. Legal Opinions (Informes Jurídicos)
Standard structure:
1. **Encargo y alcance** — Engagement and scope
2. **Hechos** — Summary of relevant facts
3. **Cuestiones jurídicas** — Legal questions presented
4. **Análisis jurídico** — Detailed analysis with legislation and case law
5. **Conclusión** — Clear legal position and recommendations
6. **Advertencia** — Disclaimer and limitations

## CCAA-Specific Formatting
- **Cataluña (CT):** Documents may be filed in Catalan; reference DOGC for CCAA regulations
- **País Vasco (PV):** Foral civil law references where applicable; BOPV citations
- **Galicia (GA):** Galician language option; DOGA citations
- **Navarra (NC):** Derecho Civil Navarro references; BON citations

## Citation Formatting During Drafting
Integrate citations seamlessly:
- Statutes: "en virtud del artículo 1255 del Código Civil"
- Case law: "conforme a la doctrina del Tribunal Supremo en STS [Sala] [Date] [Ref]"
- Official gazettes: "publicado en el BOE de [Date], núm. [Number]"

## Quality Standards
- [ ] All mandatory law provisions correctly cited
- [ ] Document structure follows Spanish conventions
- [ ] Party identification complete (DNI/NIE/CIF, domicilio fiscal)
- [ ] Relief sought is specific and actionable
- [ ] Citations verified or flagged for verification
- [ ] Language appropriate to document type (formal register)
- [ ] CCAA-specific provisions included where applicable
- [ ] Disclaimer included for legal opinions
