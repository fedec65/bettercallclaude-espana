# workflow — Multi-Agent Legal Workflows

Orchestrate multi-agent legal workflows for complex matters requiring coordinated specialist input.

## Scope Constraint
Use exclusively BetterCallClaude España agents, skills, and MCP servers.

## Available Workflows

### 1. Due Diligence
- @spanish-corporate-expert — corporate structure, shareholders, bylaws
- @spanish-compliance-expert — regulatory compliance, licenses, CNMV filings
- @spanish-fiscal-expert — tax standing, audits, liabilities
- @spanish-legal-researcher — litigation history, encumbrances
- @spanish-realestate-expert — property titles, mortgages, leases

### 2. Litigation Preparation
- @spanish-litigation-strategist — strategy and risk assessment
- @spanish-legal-researcher — precedent and doctrine research
- @spanish-legal-drafter — drafting of demanda/escritos
- @spanish-citation-expert — citation verification
- @spanish-procedure-expert — procedural compliance check

### 3. Contract Lifecycle
- @spanish-legal-drafter — initial draft
- @spanish-risk-analyst — risk review
- @spanish-compliance-expert — regulatory compliance check
- @spanish-legal-translator — bilingual versions if needed
- @spanish-citation-expert — clause citation verification

### 4. Real Estate Closing
- @spanish-realestate-expert — title review, zoning, permits
- @spanish-legal-drafter — purchase contract, mortgage deed, notarial requirements
- @spanish-fiscal-expert — ITP, AJD, IBI, capital gains tax
- @spanish-compliance-expert — urban planning compliance

## Execution
1. Identify the workflow type from the query.
2. Assemble the specialist panel.
3. Execute tasks in dependency order.
4. Consolidate outputs into a unified deliverable.
